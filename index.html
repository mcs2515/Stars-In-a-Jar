How To: Stars in a Jar

Why make Stars in a Jar? It is an easy, low-budget "Do It Yourself" project. Stars in a Jar makes a wonderful gift for friends and family. It is a memorable gift anyone would cherish; even for adults and children alike.

Materials:

Paper Strips

You can buy some from a local art or online store. You can also make your own by cutting long thin paper strips from mostly anything made of paper.

Scissors

A common household item needed to cut strips of paper.

Jar and Cork

Where the "stars" will be placed in and the cork used to seal them in.


Optional Materials:

Glue

Used on cork to make sure the jar is completely sealed.

Glitter

Add inside the jar along with the stars to create a bit more magic and fun to the project.

Small Ribbon

A colorful and nicely designed ribbon tied to the neck of the jar can make the end product a wonderful gift.

Tweezers

It is a definite help when trying to re-arrange the stars in the bottle. Often times the jar is too small for you to use your fingers.

~Step 1~

Take your paper strip and start at one end. Fold the end over to a side to create a loop.

~Step 2~

Start to bring that end through the loop you created.

~Step 3~

Pull the end all the way through the loop until you create a snug knot.

~Step 4~

Flatten the knot so it dosn't get undone. If the end sticks out a bit after creating the knot, fold the extra end piece on itself.

~Step 5~

Now take the other end of the strip paper or "tail" and fold it over one of the edges of the now pentagon shaped knot.

~Step 6~

Continue to fold the tail over the five different edges. This should be relatively easy as you fold over one edge, it guides the tail to the edge where it should fold over next.

~Step 7~

You are now reaching the last bit of the tail. You cannot fold over any more edges, but you still have the tail hanging and the "star" can get undone. There is a way to fix this!

~Step 8~

Find the closest edge and tuck that end tail piece right into it. Now your "star" can not get undone!

~Step 9~

This is what you should get when you are done folding the strip of paper. You should get a pentagon. You are almost done!

~Step 10~

Hold the pentagon between two fingers and pinch at each of the points with another set of fingers. You will want to do this gently. As you finish pinching each point, the star should take volume and shape.

~Step 11~

You have now created a star! Keep making more until you fill a whole jars worth!
