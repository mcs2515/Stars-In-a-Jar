Project 1 Explanation

Why I chose Stars in a Jar:

I wanted to create a relatively easy to follow tutorial on how to make origami stars. I was first introduced to this by my friend who gave me a paper star, and I thought it was wonderful. This is why I am now creating a tutorial that teaches anyone how to make a paper star.

My Above and Beyond:

I added an image in the background that gets repeated on the y and x axis of the webpage. I also added transparency to a few of the dive boxes on each page. I had fun changing the bullet shape to something that was not already built in HTML/CSS.

How I could Improve:

I could have spent more time on color scheme. I should of planned out different design layouts and tested different colors together; that way I could have more options and an easier time on coding the look of the website. I also could have done something more with the links during responsive design. I would have liked to make them into buttons or something.
