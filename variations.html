Stars in a Jar: Variations

Here are some variations of what your Star in a Jar could Look Like. All you need are paper strips and a jar to start! There are a ton of options for the design and color for the paper; even glitter or metallic paper. There are also a ton of different size and shape jars and bottles to fill them up. Tie a ribbon on the neck of the jar to give your Stars in a Jar a finishing touch!
